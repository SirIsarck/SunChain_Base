## Problem

Over 600M Africans lack reliable electricity access.

# Key challenges:

- Grid power is expensive, unstable, and centralized
- Rural communities rely on generators with high fuel cost
- Energy billing lacks transparency and trust
- Community micro-grids struggle with revenue coordination
- Operators have no reliable data for maintenance or planning

There is no open infrastructure that:
tracks energy usage transparently
automates billing and payments
enables trust among users, operators, and contributors

## Solution ‚Äî SunChain on Base

SunChain is a DePIN community energy network that connects solar hubs to smart meters and records verified energy usage on-chain on Base.
Our system provides:

- Transparent, tamper-resistant usage records
- Stablecoin micro-payments via smart contracts
- Automated revenue sharing among stakeholders
- Offline-first metering for rural communities
- SunChain demonstrates a utility-driven DePIN model, where value is created from real-world energy services.

## Why Build on Base

Base offers:
- Low-cost micro-transactions for energy billing
- Ethereum-secured settlement
Supportive DePIN ecosystem
- Scalability for multi-community deployments
- Developer-friendly infrastructure and analytics

Base allows SunChain to:

* anchor meter identity and data proofs on-chain
* automate stablecoin billing and settlement
* enable transparent community revenue distribution


## How the Smart Meter & IoT System Works

Operational Architecture

1. Smart hub meter measures:
user energy consumption
uptime & load performance
battery and distribution status
2. Data is processed by local AI agent:
anomaly detection
predictive maintenance flags
per-user billing logic
3. When connectivity is available:
aggregated data summary is hashed
proof is submitted on Base
4. Smart contract verifies:
timestamp
meter identity
data integrity
5. Billing + revenue share is executed automatically.

System supports offline operation, with sync-later capability.


## Reward & Incentive Model (Utility-First)

SunChain does not launch a token at pilot stage.

Incentives are based on:

* node uptime & reliability
* validated energy contribution
* data accuracy & performance

Revenue share beneficiaries:

* Hub operator
* Maintenance team
* Community partners / backers

Payments are executed via escrow-based smart contracts on Base.

All records are auditable and provable.
